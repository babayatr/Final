Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tim Cologne?

Tim Cologne is a premium cologne sample provider that allows you to explore and discover fragrances without committing to a full-size bottle. Our samples are carefully curated to give you the best experience possible.

How do I use the samples?

Each sample comes in a small, easy-to-use vial. Simply apply to your wrist or neck and experience how the fragrance evolves throughout the day. This allows you to determine if a scent is right for you before purchasing a full bottle.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. Every fragrance we offer is 100% authentic, sourced directly from the brands or trusted distributors. We take authenticity seriously so you can explore confidently.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We offer worldwide shipping so fragrance enthusiasts everywhere can enjoy our samples. Shipping times may vary depending on your location.

How long does shipping take?

Domestic shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while international shipping may take 7-15 business days depending on customs. We provide tracking information for every order.

Can I return or exchange samples?

Because these are fragrance samples, we cannot accept returns for hygiene reasons. However, if your order is damaged or incorrect, please contact us and we will resolve it promptly.

Do you offer gift sets?

Yes! We have curated gift sets that make the perfect present for fragrance lovers. They include multiple samples and are packaged beautifully.

How often do you add new fragrances?

We regularly update our collection with new and trending fragrances to keep your exploration fresh and exciting. Follow us to stay updated on new arrivals.

Can I buy a full bottle after trying a sample?

Yes! Once you’ve found a fragrance you love, we provide links or guidance on where to purchase full-size bottles from trusted sources.
